Our Products

Research / Databases

LexisNexis provides access to some four billion documents from over 35,000 authorative sources. This reliable information will give you a clear picture of the trends and developments of recent years, for example in the financial-economic, legal and social fields.

Our products are based on the combination of a huge database and the most powerful search technology. Each product gives fast and targeted access to a unique selection of sources chosen according to a specific field of application.

New Nexis.com - Based on our new global platform, the largest database of news and business information in the world, all from a single login.

Lexis.com - Is the total research product of choice in the legal market. With the advantage of functionality such as LexisNexis Search Advisor, case summaries and points of law become quick and simple to find.

Risk and Compliance - Protect your business from financial or operational risk with this enhanced Due Diligence product or Continuous Monitoring solution.

LexisNexis Publisher - LexisNexis Publisher is a powerful tool that allows you to publish articles form 11000+ international news sources onto your organizations intranet site or web site. This useful monitoring service gives you complete control of delivering information through out your organization.

TotalPatents - Most comprehensive and easy to use patent research system available anywhere. With new exclusive 22 authorities full-text patent sources and an additional 74 authorities coupled with Patent Family, Legal Status, pdf's and many more features.
